Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used, and disclosed by Dunje Game « We ». Please read our privacy policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personally Identifiable Information in accordance with our apps.

What personal information do we collect from the people that visit our apps ?

We do not collect any information from visitors of our apps. Our apps, games and services are meant for general audiences and do not knowingly collect personal information from adults or children.

Ads Providers

The ads providers we currently use are :

Google Advertising Products
AppLovin Corp.
Fractional Media
LifeStreet Corporation
Chartboost

Please refer to the privacy policy from those providers to know more about how they treat your data, what data they collect and other related information. Those providers may show you ads according to your interests to customize ads for you.

We ask, on their behalf, for your consent to see personalized ads through a message shown to you when you open our apps for the first time. If you do not consent you will be shown non-personalized ads that are not based on a user’s past behavior. They are targeted using contextual information, including coarse (such as city-level) geo-targeting based on current location, and content on the current site or app or current query terms.

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

We ask new users to provide their age in an attempt to better protect younger. Users under 16 years old will be shown non-personalized ads.

You have the right to change your mind and withdraw your consent. For opting out from receiving interest-based or targeted ads through all ads providers, please see opt out options and below : Turn on “Limit Ad Tracking” on your iOS device or “Opt Out of Interest-Based Ads” on your Android device or choose “Ads Settings” within the settings or information of our apps.

You also have the right to review what data the ad providers hold about you, check how those data are used and ask ad providers to permanently erase your personal data.

Cookies

Strictly necessary cookies may be stored in your device for frequency capping, aggregated ad reporting, and to combat fraud and abuse. If you continue to use our apps, we will assume that you agree to the use of such cookies.

Leaderboard

Some of our apps rely on Game Center or Google Play Services to store your game score and progress and to access the world-wide leaderboards. Any score the app submits to Game Center or Google Play Services on your behalf may contain information on the country from which it was submitted to give the scoring leaderboards a more global meaning. Data submitted to Game Center is subject to the Apple Privacy Policy. Data submitted to Google Play Services is subject to the Google Privacy Policy.
