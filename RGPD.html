Dunje Game Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used, and disclosed by Dunje Game « We ». Please read our privacy policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personally Identifiable Information in accordance with our apps.

What personal information do we collect from the people that visit our apps ?

We do not collect information from visitors of our apps. Our apps, games and services are meant for general audiences and do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Ads Providers

The ads providers we currently use are Google and Chartboost. Those providers may show you ads according to your interests to tailor ads for you. We ask, on their behalf, for your consent to see relevant ads. Please refer to Terms&Conditions/Privacy Notes from those providers to know more about how they treat your data, what data they collect and other related information. Google: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites Chartboost: https://answers.chartboost.com/en-us/articles/200780269 If you do not consent, you will be shown less relevant ads that are not based on a user’s past behavior. They are targeted using contextual information, including coarse (such as city-level) geo-targeting based on current location, and content on the current site or app or current query terms.

Leaderboard

Some of our apps rely on Game Center to store your game score and progress and to access the world-wide leaderboards. Any score the app submits to Game Center on your behalf may contain information on the country from which it was submitted to give the scoring leaderboards a more global meaning. Data submitted to Game Center is subject to the Apple Privacy Policy. You can always choose to disable Game Center from your device Settings or remove our specific app from Game Center using the built-in Game Center app.
